# Together

## Details
Together is a two-player top-down real-time action adventure game made in Godot and Piskel. Learn about the Dullworld and Mystia, their history together, and why their peoples parted ways. Could these peoples ever be reunited? You play as the Dullian archer Ryl and the Mystical wizard Elvyria, fighting through enemies to find one of the many Sacred Gates—gateways between the Dullworld and Mystia. As you progress, you gain upgrades like the Tri Bow (shoot 3 arrows at once) and the Armor of Thorns (touching enemies hurts them).
